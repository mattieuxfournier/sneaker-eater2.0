Movement

In the side scroller shooter game sneaker eater you use the little arrow keys at the bottom of your keyboard. This makes this game super relaxing to play for casual gamers.


Abilities


In Sneaker Eater you have 2 common abilities those being the ability to shoot, sprint and the ability to jump. If you want to shoot then press the left mouse button, if youd like to sprint press shift, if you want to jump press the spacebar.


Goals

The goal of the 2D sidescroller Sneaker Eater is to escape from the shoe mall by finding secret passages and hidden doors. Your kills are added to the top of the screen once you escape
